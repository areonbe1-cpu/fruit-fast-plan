90-Day Detox Protocol

The Fruit Fast
Master Plan

Physical Detox · Deep Cleanse · Full Reset

Designed for a woman working a physical job 3–4 days/week with access to tropical, citrus & berry fruits. This plan prioritizes cellular detox, sustained energy, and safe completion of a full 90-day fruit fast.

The Structure

Three Phases of the Fast

I
Days 1 – 30

The Purge

Body transitions from processed food to fruit sugar. Expect detox symptoms — headaches, fatigue, emotional releases, cravings. This is the hardest phase. Do not quit here.

Focus: Hydration · Electrolytes · Rest as much as possible
II
Days 31 – 60

The Flow

Body adapts. Energy stabilises. Skin begins to clear. Mental clarity increases. Cravings reduce significantly. Work performance becomes more manageable.

Focus: Variety · Calorie sufficiency · Consistent routine
III
Days 61 – 90

The Refinement

Deep cellular detox. Body is lean, organs are resting. Energy is clean. This is where the real transformation sets in — physically and internally.

Focus: Completion · Breaking fast protocol · Transition plan
Key Mindset Rule Days 3–7 and Days 21–28 are typically the hardest. Prepare her mentally for those windows. They pass.
Daily Structure

Work Day vs Rest Day Schedule

🔴 Work Day (3–4x per week)
5:30 AM
Wake + Morning Water 1 large glass warm water with fresh-squeezed lemon. This wakes the lymphatic system and prepares digestion.
6:00 AM
Pre-Work Fuel — High Sugar Fruit 2–3 bananas OR a large bowl of mango/papaya. High calorie, fast fuel for physical labour. Add dates if available (5–6 pieces).
9–10 AM
Mid-Morning Snack Watermelon (large portion) OR coconut water if available. This is hydration + sugar replenishment. Do not skip this on work days.
12–1 PM
Midday Meal — Biggest Meal of the Day Large fruit bowl: mango + papaya + banana + orange. Aim for 4–6 pieces of fruit. Volume matters here — she cannot undereat on work days.
3–4 PM
Afternoon Fuel Berries (strawberries, blueberries) OR citrus (2 oranges / 1 grapefruit). Helps avoid the afternoon energy crash.
6–7 PM
Evening Wind-Down Meal Lighter fruit — papaya, melon, or grapes. Easier to digest as the body slows. Avoid heavy bananas at night during work days.
8–9 PM
Final Water + Rest Water with lemon or plain. No more eating after 8:30 PM to give the body overnight detox time. Aim for 9 PM sleep on work days.
🟢 Rest Day (3–4x per week)
7–8 AM
Slow Wake + Water Warm lemon water. No rush. Let the body ease into the day. Rest days are detox acceleration days — the body works hard when you're still.
9 AM
Light Breakfast 2 oranges or a grapefruit + small bunch of grapes. Lower sugar intake than work days since energy demand is lower.
12 PM
Midday Meal Watermelon (2–3 large slices) OR papaya bowl. Watermelon is ideal on rest days — deeply hydrating, kidney-cleansing, low effort to digest.
3–4 PM
Afternoon Snack Berries or 1–2 mangoes. Optional — only eat if hungry. Rest days may naturally reduce appetite as detox deepens.
6 PM
Evening Meal Small mixed fruit bowl. Keep it light. The body does its deepest detox work overnight — a lighter dinner supports that process.
7:30 PM
Done Eating + Wind Down Water only after this point. Rest days close eating earlier. Stretch, breathe, sleep early if possible.

What to Expect

Detox Symptoms & Solutions

Symptom When It Hits What to Do
Headaches Days 3–10 Drink more water. Add lemon. Rest. This is sugar/caffeine withdrawal.
Extreme fatigue Days 5–14 Normal. Body is redirecting energy to detox. Sleep more. Reduce work output if possible.
Dizziness / lightheadedness Any time Eat immediately — bananas + dates. This is low blood sugar. Increase calorie intake that day.
Emotional releases (crying, anger) Days 7–21 Allow it. The body stores emotion in tissue. Detox releases it. This is part of the process.
Skin breakouts Weeks 2–4 Toxins exiting through the skin. Do not treat with products. Keep skin clean and let it pass.
Strong cravings Weeks 1–3 Eat more fruit — volume, not variety. Cravings are often just insufficient calories or sugar dip.
Muscle cramps Work days esp. Potassium deficiency. Eat bananas + coconut water immediately. Add dates to daily intake.
Frequent urination / loose stools Week 1–2 Normal elimination. Body flushing. Stay hydrated. Not a problem unless accompanied by pain.
Day 91 Onwards

How to Break the Fast

Breaking a 90-day fruit fast incorrectly can cause serious digestive distress. The gut has been in a simplified state for 3 months. Transition must be gradual.

1
Week 1 After — Add Raw Vegetables Cucumber, lettuce, celery, tomato alongside fruit. No cooked food yet. Let the digestive enzymes wake up gradually.
2
Week 2 After — Add Steamed Vegetables Lightly steamed greens, sweet potato, squash. Continue eating fruit as well. Introduce one new food every 2 days to identify any reaction.
3
Week 3 After — Add Legumes & Grains Small portions of lentils, beans, brown rice. Chew thoroughly. Portions should be half of what feels like enough.
4
Week 4+ — Return to Full Diet Gradually Reintroduce foods one at a time. Dairy and processed food should be the last things reintroduced — or left behind entirely.
Do Not Do This Do not eat a large cooked meal on Day 91. Do not reintroduce meat before Week 3–4 minimum. Do not eat processed or fried food in the first month after. The gut wall is fresh — protect it.
Non-Negotiables

Water & Daily Essentials

Every Single Day

Water Intake

Rest days: minimum 2.5 litres. Work days: minimum 3.5 litres. Add lemon to at least 2 glasses daily. Water is half the detox — do not underestimate it.

Morning Ritual

Lemon Water First

Every morning before any fruit — warm water + fresh-squeezed lemon. This activates the lymphatic system, alkalises the gut, and begins the day's detox process.

Avoid Completely

Zero Tolerance List

Coffee. Tea (except plain herbal). Alcohol. Soft drinks. Supplements (unless directed by doctor). Chewing gum. Anything with artificial sweeteners or flavouring.
